# Annual-Store-sales-report-and-dashboard-Excel-
The 2025 store report shows strong early-year sales, with women driving 64% of orders. Delivery success is high at 92%, and seniors form the largest customer group. Maharashtra leads in sales, while Amazon is the top channel. Overall performance is stable with clear growth areas.
The Store Annual Report 2025 highlights overall business performance across sales, customer behavior, and regional insights. Sales peaked between February and May, while women contributed the majority of purchases at 64%. Order operations were highly efficient, with 92% successful deliveries and minimal returns or cancellations. Senior customers formed the largest buying group, followed by adults. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh emerged as the top-performing states. Amazon remained the strongest sales channel, followed by Myntra and Flipkart. Overall, the report shows strong customer engagement, high delivery success, and clear trends for future strategic planning.
